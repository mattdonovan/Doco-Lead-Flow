Siding

What's driving this project? → Visible damage or rot / Storm damage / Just outdated — ready for an upgrade / Selling the home soon / Not sure, need an opinion

What's the main reason? → Drafty or poor insulation / Condensation or fogging between panes / Damaged / won't open or close / Storm damage / Just upgrading for curb appeal

What's the main issue? → Overflowing during rain / Sagging or pulling away / Visible damage or holes / I don't have gutters yet / Full replacement — they're just old
